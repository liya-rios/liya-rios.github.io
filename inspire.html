What makes a hero?

content

As someone who values advocacy and using art as a medium to spread awareness,I find Sister Corita's ability to break artistic and societal barriers throughout the 20th century beyond inspiring.

Her work was not only unique in style and medium, but encouraged conversations on the intersection of race, poverty, and social injustice occurring throughout this time.

I continue to think about the impact of Sister Corita's work and consider how my own work can hold a similar influence on our world.
